Frail

What do you do when your heart breaks?

When you hear the crack within the walls,
And feel the blood spill out as it beats.

Rejected

Alone

Misunderstood

Ignored

I ask,
I cry,
I shout and still…
I remain unheard.

What’s louder than your silence?
Nothing.

If only you would hear me the way I hear...
Silence.

See the hidden parts of me,
And turn your head away.

Am I not enough?

If I show you all that I am,
Will you turn back toward me,
And show me more of you too?

Am I not deserving?

If I cannot be seen,
Perhaps,
I am not here.




Nya Wira
